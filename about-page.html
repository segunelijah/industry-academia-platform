About Collegal
What We Do
Through our industry, we empower organizations to solve complex problems and see the true value of ideas.

Every day we help people find, capture, and share ideas to improve our world. Whether it’s

widening access to clean water in developing countries or expanding space research and

exploration, Wazoku supercharges your ideas into reality.

We have two decades of experience across multiple industries. We’ve worked with everyone,

from global corporations to local teams, to harness the innate potential in everyone to be an

innovator.
Our Values
Change the world, one idea at a time
Passionate, Customer-focused, Collaborative, Accountable

Our mission is to enable problem-solving at scale around the world: no matter how big or small

the problem you’re dealing with, Wazoku can help you solve it with industry-leading tools and

experience.

Our people are what truly set us apart

We don’t only advocate for collaboration and growth for our customers – these are methods for how

we work and develop together, too

We’ve built an incredible and talented team who love what they do and where they work. We couldn’t

be more proud of how the company has grown and changed since its inception.

As a founder-led business, Collegal is more like a family than a top-down hierarchy. Our values are

A deep commitment to diversity

Making positive social impacts – not just through our work but through the way we run the

business
A commitment to a more regenerative future
A passionate belief in supporting and nurturing our team

Become a solver and you will:

Make a Positive Impact

Our Open Talent crowd solvers have tackled some of the world’s most pressing problems, from

facilitating access to clean water to passive solar devices designed to attract and kill malaria-

carrying mosquitoes.

Promote Yourself and Earn Rewards

We’ve awarded more than $60 million to thousands of brilliant solvers from around the world. The

average award amount is around $20,000, but some customers offer awards of $100,000 or more.

However, it’s not just about the financial reward: your accomplishment is guaranteed to attract

attention on your CV, social media profiles, and Community profile .

Exercise Your Brain

Challenges are real problems requiring concentration, critical thinking, research and creativity.

Developing a solution to a global issue is incredibly rewarding and an unparalleled mental

workout.
